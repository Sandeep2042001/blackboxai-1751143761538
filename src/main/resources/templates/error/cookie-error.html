How to Enable Cookies

Google Chrome

  1. Click the menu icon (⋮)
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Click "Privacy and security"
  4. Click "Cookies and other site data"
  5. Enable "Allow sites to save and read cookie data"

Mozilla Firefox

  1. Click the menu button (☰)
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Click "Privacy & Security"
  4. Under "Enhanced Tracking Protection"
  5. Choose "Standard" or "Custom"

Safari

  1. Click "Safari" in the menu
  2. Select "Preferences"
  3. Go to "Privacy" tab
  4. Uncheck "Block all cookies"
  5. Allow "blingbag.co.in"

Microsoft Edge

  1. Click the menu icon (⋯)
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Click "Cookies and site permissions"
  4. Click "Manage and delete cookies"
  5. Enable "Allow sites to save and read cookie data"
